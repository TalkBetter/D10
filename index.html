Part B ｜ 植物成長四階段

🌱 四大階段關鍵字

First
seed 種子
Next
sprout 萌芽
Then
seedling 幼苗 bud 花苞
Last
bloom 開花 flowers 花 fruit 果實

✏️ 例句組合 ①

  • 首先，把種子種在土裡。
    First, plant the seeds in the soil.
  • 接著，種子發芽，長出小小的根、葉子和莖。
    Next, the seed sprouts with tiny roots, leaves, and a stem.
  • 然後，它長成一株幼苗。
    Then, it grows into a seedling.
  • 最後，植物長出花和果實。
    Last, the plant grows flowers and fruit.

✏️ 例句組合 ②

  • 首先，一顆種子睡在土裡。
    First, a seed sleeps in the soil.
  • 接著，一株小小的嫩芽從種子裡長出來。
    Next, a small sprout comes out of the seed.
  • 然後，一個綠色花苞長在高高的植物上。
    Then, a green bud grows on the tall plant.
  • 最後，向日葵在陽光下燦爛綻放。
    Last, the sunflower blooms bright in the sunshine.

Part C ｜ Venus the Flytrap 捕蠅草 Venus

English Story

One day, Mason had a Venus flytrap named Venus.

A flytrap is supposed to eat bugs, but Venus would not eat flies, bees, or spiders.

Mason and his mom were worried because Venus was getting weak.

They took Venus to Papa Longo's pizza shop for help.

At the shop, Venus smelled a fresh pizza and thought it smelled delicious.

Then a fly landed on the pizza.

Papa Longo gave Venus a little piece of pizza with the fly on it.

Snap! Venus finally ate it and felt happy.

中文故事

有一天，Mason 有一株名叫 Venus 的捕蠅草。

捕蠅草本來應該要吃昆蟲，但是 Venus 不肯吃蒼蠅、蜜蜂或蜘蛛。

Mason 和他的媽媽很擔心，因為 Venus 變得越來越虛弱。

他們把 Venus 帶到 Papa Longo 的披薩店尋求幫助。

在店裡，Venus 聞到剛出爐的披薩，覺得聞起來很香。

接著，一隻蒼蠅停在披薩上。

Papa Longo 給 Venus 一小塊上面有蒼蠅的披薩。

啪！Venus 終於把它吃掉了，而且感到很開心。

❓ 題目和答案 Questions & Answers

Q1. Who are the characters in the story? 故事裡有哪些角色？
A: The characters in the story are Mason, Venus, Mason's mom, and Papa Longo. 故事裡的角色有 Mason、Venus、Mason 的媽媽，還有 Papa Longo。
Q2. Why were Mason and his mom worried about Venus? 為什麼 Mason 和他的媽媽會擔心 Venus？
A: Mason and his mom were worried because Venus would not eat bugs and she was getting weak. Mason 和他的媽媽很擔心，因為 Venus 不肯吃昆蟲，而且她變得越來越虛弱。
Q3. What did Venus finally eat, and why did she eat it? Venus 最後吃了什麼？她為什麼吃它？
A: Venus finally ate a piece of pizza with a fly on it because it smelled delicious. Venus 最後吃了一塊上面有蒼蠅的披薩，因為那聞起來很香。
